@@@@@@@@About Pomodoro@@@@@@@@@@@

INVENTED BY : Francesco Cirillo

INVENTED IN : late 1980s

This technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals,

traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks of 5 minutes.

Each interval is known as a pomodoro Sessions.

The technique has been widely popularized by dozens of apps and 

websites providing timers and instructions.

Source : https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pomodoro_Technique
